Ecogenesis

This project investigates the intersection between native flora and fauna and the natural processes of life and death of which are threatened by the negligence of human behaviour. A supplementary part of this artwork is an information booklet with fact sheets about each of the plants in the main work including conservation status, Aboriginal uses, and distribution.

Sasha Thurkettle, 2024, Ecogenesis, A1, coloured pencil on paper.
